Overview

Sky Castle is a South Korean television series that delves into the lives of wealthy families residing in a luxurious residential area called Sky Castle. The show revolves around the intense competition and pressures faced by parents to ensure their children gain admission to prestigious universities, particularly in the fiercely competitive environment of South Korea.
