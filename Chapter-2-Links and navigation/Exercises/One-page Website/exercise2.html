Ratings: 4.9/5 (Viki) | Seasons: 1 | Release Year: 2018

Official Website

Overview

Sky Castle is a South Korean television series that delves into the lives of wealthy families residing in a luxurious residential area called Sky Castle. The show revolves around the intense competition and pressures faced by parents to ensure their children gain admission to prestigious universities, particularly in the fiercely competitive environment of South Korea.

Character Description

  • Han Seo-jin: A determined and ambitious woman who will go to any lengths to maintain her family's status.
  • No Seung-hye: Seo-jin's friend and rival, who faces her own challenges in balancing family and societal expectations.
  • Kim Eun-hye: A former doctor turned housewife, Eun-hye struggles with the pressures placed on her daughter to succeed academically.
  • Jin Jin-hee: A divorcee and single mother who desires success for her son, but faces obstacles due to her lower social status.
  • Kang Joon-sang: A mysterious tutor who enters the lives of the Sky Castle families and brings about unforeseen consequences.
